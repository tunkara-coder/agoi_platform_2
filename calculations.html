AGOI Scoring Framework

The Africa Green Opportunity Index uses a weighted multi-pillar approach:

Pillar Weight Description
Policy & Governance 25% Quality of green policies and regulatory frameworks
Sectoral Potential 30% Availability of natural resources and infrastructure
Finance Ecosystem 25% Availability of green finance and investment
Human Capital 20% Workforce skills and inclusive participation
