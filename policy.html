Policy & Governance Pillar

About This Pillar

The Policy & Governance pillar evaluates the quality and effectiveness of green policies, regulatory frameworks, and institutional capacity that support sustainable development.

Key Indicators:
  • National green growth strategies and targets
  • Environmental regulations and enforcement
  • Climate policy frameworks
  • Institutional capacity and coordination
  • Transparency and stakeholder engagement
Assessment Methodology:

This pillar is assessed through a combination of policy document analysis, regulatory quality assessments, and expert surveys evaluating implementation effectiveness.
