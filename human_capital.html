Human Capital Pillar

About This Pillar

The Human Capital pillar evaluates the availability of skilled workforce, education systems, training programs, and inclusive participation opportunities in the green economy.

Key Indicators:
  • Green skills education and training
  • Workforce readiness for green jobs
  • Research and innovation capacity
  • Inclusive employment opportunities
  • Technical and vocational training
Assessment Methodology:

This pillar combines education statistics, labor market analysis, training program assessments, and skills gap analysis to evaluate human capital development for green growth.
