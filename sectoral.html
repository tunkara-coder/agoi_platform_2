Sectoral Potential Pillar

About This Pillar

The Sectoral Potential pillar assesses the availability and quality of natural resources, infrastructure, and economic conditions that enable green growth across key sectors.

Key Indicators:
  • Renewable energy potential (solar, wind, hydro, geothermal)
  • Sustainable agriculture and land use
  • Green infrastructure development
  • Circular economy opportunities
  • Green technology adoption
Assessment Methodology:

This pillar combines geospatial data analysis, resource mapping, infrastructure assessments, and sectoral economic analysis to evaluate green growth potential.
